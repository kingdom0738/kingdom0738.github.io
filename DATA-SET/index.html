DATA

In a mission to foster the use of data for research. If a useful dataset is missing, please let me know.

I. Firm Behavior

  • World Management Survey: Cross-country survey of management behavior. By Bloom, Sadun and Van Reenen.

  • Firm-Level Lobbying Activity in the U.S.: The best firm-level lobbying dataset available in the world. By In Song Kim.

  • Enterprise Survey: Cross-country surveys of firm behavior. By the World Bank.

  • Antidumping: Firm-specific data and documentation on U.S. antidumping. By Blonigen.

  • Amadeus: European firm-level characteristics. By Bureau van Dijk.

  • Orbis: Global firm-level characteristics. By Bureau van Dijk.

II. Macro

  • Industry-level Production Dataset for the U.S.: By the NBER.

  • Cross-Country Productivity: Link to Penn World Tables, 10-Sector Database, EU Klems, among others. By the GGDC.

  • Historical Development: Cross-country long time series on economic growth. Includes Maddisonâ€™s data. By the GGDC.

  • World Development Indicators: Compilation of cross-country comparable data on development. By the World Bank.

  • Cross-Country Historical Inequality: By a team of international researchers.

  • World Input-Output Database: By researchers from Groningen.

III. Institutions

  • Doing Business Survey: Cross-country survey of business regulations. By the World Bank.

  • Worldwide Governance Indicators: Cross-country governance survey to citizens, firms and experts. By Kaufmann and Kray.

IV. International Trade

  • Exporter Dynamics Dataset: Cross-country characteristics of firm-level exporter dynamics. By the World Bank.

  • Harmonized International Trade at Product and Country-level: By CEPII.

  • Trade Elasticities: By Broda and Weinstein.

  • Non-Tariff Barriers: By Trefler.

V. Politics

  • Campaign finance in the U.S.: By the Federal Electoral Commission of the U.S.

  • Nominates Scores: Historical dataset of congress member vote behavior in the U.S. By Poole and Rosenthal.

VI. Diverse Purposes

  • World Bank Datasets: Development cross-country datasets.

  • Global Barometer: Cross-country surveys of political, economical and social behavior.

  • NBER Datasets: Macro, finance, trade, labor health, demographics. You name it.

  • CEPII Datasets: Macro, trade, geography, productivity.

  • GGDC Datasets: Macro, productivity, global value chains, historical development. The best source for global macro datasets.

  • ICPSR Datasets: Massive and collective compilation of all purpose datasets.
